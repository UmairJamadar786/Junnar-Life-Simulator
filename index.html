About the project

"Junnar Life Simulator" is a forthcoming, high-fidelity simulation crafted to bring the streets, landmarks and everyday life of Junnar, Pune to virtual life. The current page presents a controlled demo snapshot and a progress summary for interested players and collaborators.

Lead designer:
Sayyed Mohammad Umair Jamadar
Development partners:
  • Sayyed Sahil
  • Sayyed Ali Aafaq
  • Sayyed Tasdiq

Status: Work in progress — design & systems integration phase

The ambition of the project is to deliver an immersive, realistic simulation featuring:

  • Accurate map geometry inspired by real-world Junnar, Pune (landmarks and road topology).
  • Authentic Indian vehicles and driving mechanics — two-wheelers such as Splendor and Hero Honda and popular SUVs such as Thar and Fortuner.
  • High-detail environmental assets, local architecture and contextual ambient audio.

Please note: this page is a public demo to demonstrate progress and system requirements only. The complete game experience and final content will be distributed when the development milestone is reached.

Official note: We appreciate the community interest. Our team is focused on realistic physics, faithful representation of local geography, and optimized performance across eligible systems. Your feedback at this stage helps us prioritize features and stability.
